Work Experience

Tutoring

Arabic Tutor

February 2017 - September 2017

I privately tutored Arabic and Islamic studies to two children once a week. We went through 5 new words every week, using flash cards and other games to help them with revision and memorisation. I read them simplified stories explaining Islamic history, as well as teaching the traditions and beliefs of Islam with the help of Islamic textbooks used in schools. I prepared games and exercises in advance to help them with their learning, and learned methods to keep them focused and interested in their subjects.

English Tutor

August 2016 - October 2016

I also assisted two Egyptian children with their English homework as well as everyday English vocabulary. I prepared games and exercises that would make their learning experience fun and less frustrating. I made them more comfortable with speaking English by introducing easy to read children's books, and English mobile games to play in their spare time. I would also assist them with completing their English, and sometimes math, homework from school.
